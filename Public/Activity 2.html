Candice's Savory Tuna Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

For the filling:

  • 1 pack of Sliced Bread
  • 1 Can of Tuna
  • 1 piece of Onion
  • 1 piece of medium size of Carrot
  • half of the Cabbage
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 1 pincg of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 220 ml of Mayonnaise
  • 1 pack of BreadCrumbs
  • 2 cups of oil
  • 1 pack of cheez Whiz

Tools used:

  • Rooling Pin
  • Bowls
  • Frying Pan
  • Fork for sealing
  • Thong
  • Knife

Procedure:

  1. Place the sliced Bread in the cutting board and press it with the rolling Pin
  2. Mix the Mayonnaise, 1 can of tuna, 1 piece of sliced onion, half of sliced Cabbage, 1 pinch of salt, 1 pinch of pepper, Sliced Carrot,
  3. Place the mixture in the top of the pressed Bread
  4. Fold the filled bread in half
  5. pinch the side of the folded bread to lock it
  6. Break the egg inside the bowl
  7. Put the BreadCrumbs inside the bowl
  8. Then dip the filled pressed bread in the egg first and dip the bread in the BreadCrums
  9. Put the oil inside the pan and wait for it to heat
  10. Then fry the pie
  11. Wait for it to be cooked and remove it from the oil
  12. Place the tuna pie in the plate and ENJOY:)
